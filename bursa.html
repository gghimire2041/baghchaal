Langur Burja

"Monkey Crown" - Sacred Festival Dice Game

🏛️ Ancient Festival Tradition: Langur Burja, meaning "Monkey Crown," is Nepal's beloved dice game played during Dashain and Tihar festivals. For centuries, families and friends have gathered to roll these sacred dice, each bearing symbols of fortune and fate. This cultural treasure connects generations through the timeless joy of chance and celebration.

🎲 How to Play: Place your bets on the symbols you believe will appear. When 6 dice are rolled, count how many times your chosen symbol appears. Win 2x your bet for 2 matches, 3x for 3 matches, and so on! May the gods of fortune smile upon you!
